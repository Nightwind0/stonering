Character Classes:

 

Warrior - hand to hand combat

 

Knight - swords, axes, etc

 

Paladin - holy knight

 

Enchanter - magic

 

Ranger – specialized weapons (projectile?), specialized magic

 

Archer - projectile weapons

 

Characters:

 

Valyn - Hero - Knight Class

 

Valyn is mistrustful because his sister was kidnapped due to betrayal, bold, direct, businesslike and sometimes rude and impolite.

 

Garian - Best Friend - Archer Class

 

Garian is a friend from childhood, overly trusting to foolishness, contrasting Valyn and polite and compassionate.

 

Torriq – Friend in Goren – Ranger class

 

Friend Valyn goes to after the battle in the tombs of the elders. Very odd, always saying strange parables that don’t apply or make any sense. Generally nice and of great assistance.

 

Lorian - Sister - Paladin Class

 

Valyn’s sister, who is taken to the magical realm as a child and imprisoned. Her cellmate is an ancient paladin, who teachers her skills she knows. As a child she was sweet and loving, but as an adult she is very cold and emotionless.

 

Shaya - Lorian’s Cellmate - Paladin Class

 

An NPC ancient imprisoned paladin who teachers Lorian all that she knows about combat. Cold and emotionless, affects Lorian that way.

 

Ordimus - Good Friend - Elder

 

An NPC (non-player character) wise and kindly elder that raised the young orphaned Valyn and Lorian (until she was taken). He is killed at the beginning of the game.

 

Dreegan - Traitor – Corrupt Elder

 

An NPC Elder that gives them first quest is actually traitor that allows Valyn’s sister to be kidnapped and when they find the scroll, he attacks them with a bunch of minions.

 

Mordus - Overlord - Enchanter Class

 

Mordus is the evil overlord who is controlled by the Dragon Laeortis. He is very wild and violent, and even after he joins Valyn’s party he is always attacking first and asking questions later.

 

Laeortis - Evil Mastermind - Dragon
